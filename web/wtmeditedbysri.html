  • OUR MISSION

    Enable technology accessible to every women of all age group by organising Events, Programs and Challenges

    OUR VISION

    Make every women capable to solve their local community problem with technology

    MAKERWOMEN CHALLENGE for JUNIORS

    This is for School Girls who will solve problems using technology. Working in teams, girls find a problem in their community and build a prototype using DESIGN THINKING process. Along the way they develop their collaboration, problem solving, and leadership skills.

    MAKERWOMEN CHALLENGE for SENIORS

    This is for College Girls who will build a mobile app to solve the problem picked up by Juniors. Before to that, they learn BUILDING WEBSITE, Converting them into PWA and FRONT END/BACK END PROGRAMMING LANGUAGE of their choice.

    MAKERWOMEN CHALLENGE for WORKING LADIES

    This is for Working Professionals who will do Marketing the product/idea created by Juniors and the app developed by Seniors. They will be trained on LOGO CREATION, DIGITAL MARKETING and MAKING QUALITY PITCH/DEMO VIDEO
